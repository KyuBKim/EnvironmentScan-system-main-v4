The AI workload explosion makes this efficiency gain economically compelling for the first time.
7. **Inference**: DC power adoption in data centers will create a beachhead for broader DC infrastructure in buildings, EV charging, and renewable energy integration. The 45% copper reduction could measurably affect global copper demand projections and reshape mining investment decisions.
