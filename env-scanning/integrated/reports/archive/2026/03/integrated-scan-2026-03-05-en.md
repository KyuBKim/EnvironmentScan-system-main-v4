### 8.3 Methodology

- **Integration Method**: Cross-workflow deduplication (URL + semantic similarity) followed by unified pSST ranking with multi-WF corroboration as tiebreaker
- **Deduplication**: Signals covering the same event from different WFs merged into unified entries noting all source workflows
- **Ranking**: pSST scores from individual workflows used as base; multi-WF corroboration adds confidence uplift of 2-5 points
- **Skeleton-Fill**: integrated-report-skeleton-en.md template
- **Quality Defense**: L1 (skeleton), L2a (validate_report.py --profile integrated), L2b (validate_report_quality.py), L3 (quality review)
- **Bilingual Protocol**: EN-first generation, KO translation for final output
- **Priority Scoring**: Python priority_score_calculator.py (weights from thresholds.yaml)
